Landing page

keyboard_arrow_down

A landing page is a single web page with one clear goal. It is designed for a specific ad or link. Its job is to get a visitor to do one thing, like buy, sign up, or learn more.

How a landing page look like

Here're some major sections a landing page must have

flag

One Clear Goal

Built for a single action, like signing up or buying one product.

breaking_news

Relevant Headline

The main title must match the ad or link that brought the visitor.

center_focus_strong

Focused Content

Only information and images that support the single goal.

call_to_action

Prominent Call-to-Action

A very clear, compelling button ("Download," "Buy Now") that stands out.
